About
Youth Wakeup Radio

Youth wakeup Radio is a licensed online radio broadcasting station that runs educational programmes, relevant community issues, current affairs, and specific topics such as Economy, sports, religion (Christianity, African region, Rasta-farianism, Muslim and others), Politics and culture and entertainment programs. We are a youth -focused online radio station established in 2022 in South Africa, Limpopo broadcasting in English, Xitsonga, Sepedi(khelobedu) and Tshivenda to the Communities in South Africa (Limpopo, Giyani), Africa and worldwide, based in Limpopo the City of Giyani from the heart of N’wamankena West .
